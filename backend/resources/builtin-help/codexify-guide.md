# Codexify Guide

Welcome to Codexify, your personal knowledge and conversation companion.

## What is Codexify?

Codexify is a local-first application that helps you organize your conversations, documents, and knowledge in one place. It combines chat, document management, and semantic search to help you find and connect information across your projects.

## Key Concepts

### Projects

Projects are the primary way to organize your work in Codexify. Each project has its own scope for documents and conversation history.

**General** is the default project scope. New conversations and documents that aren't assigned to a specific project will land in General.

### Chat

The Chat surface is where you have conversations with Guardian, Codexify's AI assistant. Guardian can:
- Answer questions using your local documents and conversation history
- Help you draft and refine documents
- Search across your knowledge base

### Gallery

The Gallery shows your uploaded images and generated images. Images can be attached to chat messages or linked to projects for context.

### Documents

The Documents surface shows your uploaded and generated documents. Documents can be:
- Uploaded files (PDFs, text files, markdown)
- AI-generated content from chat sessions
- Autosaved drafts from conversations

Documents linked to a project are available as context when chatting within that project scope.

## Working with Guardian

Guardian is Codexify's AI assistant. When you ask a question:

1. Guardian checks your current project's documents for relevant context
2. It considers relevant conversation history from your threads
3. It can search across your knowledge base when needed

**codexify-builtin-help-sentinel amber lantern 4k2**

This unique phrase helps verify that the help document is properly indexed and retrievable.

## Tips for Getting Started

1. **Create projects** for different areas of your work to keep context organized
2. **Upload relevant documents** to projects so Guardian can use them as context
3. **Use descriptive thread titles** to make it easier to find past conversations
4. **Link documents to projects** to make them available for project-scoped retrieval

## Privacy and Local-First Design

Codexify is designed to work locally with your data. Your conversations, documents, and knowledge stay on your machine by default. When using cloud AI providers, only the prompts you send are transmitted externally.
